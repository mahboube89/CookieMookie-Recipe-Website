How To Cook

  • Step 1: Gather all your ingredients and ensure everything is fresh and prepped.
  • Step 2: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) for an evenly baked dish.
  • Step 3: Combine your ingredients following the recipe steps, stirring gently for a perfect mix.
  • Step 4: Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and aromatic.
  • Step 5: Let it cool slightly, serve, and savor the delightful flavors you’ve created!
